Roast Beef and Cheddar Sliders

Description

Roast Beef and Cheddar Sliders are delicious mini sandwiches that combine tender roast beef, melty cheddar cheese, and a flavorful butter sauce, all nestled in soft slider buns. These crowd-pleasing treats are easy to make and perfect for feeding a group.

Ingredients

  • 12 slider buns (preferably Hawaiian sweet rolls)
  • 1 pound thinly sliced deli roast beef
  • 8 ounces sliced sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons prepared horseradish (optional)
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Without separating the rolls, slice the entire slab of slider buns in half horizontally.
  3. In a small bowl, mix mayonnaise and horseradish (if using). Spread this mixture on the cut sides of both halves of the buns.
  4. Layer the bottom half of the buns with roast beef slices, followed by cheddar cheese. Place the top half of the buns over the cheese.
  5. In another bowl, whisk together melted butter, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, poppy seeds, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  6. Pour this butter mixture evenly over the tops of the sliders, allowing it to seep down into the sandwiches.
  7. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for about 15 minutes.
  8. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 5 minutes, or until the tops are lightly golden and the cheese is fully melted.
  9. Let the sliders cool for a few minutes before serving.
